# PROGRAM-0001 Publisher Acknowledgments

This directory contains Publisher acknowledgment records associated with `PROGRAM-0001 — AISET Program Charter`.

Publisher acknowledgments record the Publisher’s recognition of lifecycle transitions, registered versions, publication states, restrictions, and related review and registry records.

---

## 1. Directory Purpose

This directory provides traceable Publisher records for:

- Draft-to-Review transitions;
- Candidate Publication recognition;
- Approved Publication recognition;
- Reference Publication recognition;
- registration acknowledgment;
- correction, suspension, withdrawal, or supersession acknowledgment;
- Publisher readiness and publication restrictions.

A Publisher acknowledgment does not itself grant final approval unless the applicable governance process and acknowledgment explicitly establish that result.

## 5. Current Restrictions

Before Candidate Publication readiness may be established, applicable requirements include:

- dedicated security and privacy review;
- resolution or formal disposition of `NEW-001 — Foundation-stage Exception Authority`;
- lifecycle-list alignment;
- independent or second-party review;
- Candidate Publication readiness evidence;
- conflict and role disclosures;
- registry readiness;
- Publisher readiness;
- applicable artifact integrity evidence.

A later lifecycle transition requires separate review, decision, registry, and Publisher records.

---

## 6. Record Integrity

Once committed and published, a Publisher acknowledgment must not be silently modified or replaced.

Material changes require, as applicable:

- a correction acknowledgment;
- a superseding acknowledgment;
- a withdrawal or suspension record;
- another documented Publisher action.

Historical acknowledgment records must remain discoverable at their recorded paths.

Repository history may support provenance but does not replace explicit Publisher records.
