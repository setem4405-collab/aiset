# PROGRAM-0001 Reviews

This directory contains review, verification, and related lifecycle records associated with `PROGRAM-0001 — AISET Program Charter`.

The records in this directory preserve the review history, findings, decisions, verification outcomes, and lifecycle implications supporting the evolution of the Charter.

Legacy Publisher acknowledgment records `PROGRAM-0001-PUBLISHER-ACK-001` and `PROGRAM-0001-PUBLISHER-ACK-002` remain in this directory at their historical paths. New Publisher acknowledgment records are maintained in:

`program/PROGRAM-0001/publisher/`

## 1. Directory Purpose

This directory provides traceable records for:

- architectural review;
- governance review;
- normative terminology review;
- targeted verification;
- editorial review;
- security and privacy review;
- decision and approval records;
- future independent or external review.

Each review record is expected to identify, as applicable:

- the reviewed document;
- the reviewed version;
- the record identifier;
- the review scope;
- the responsible reviewer;
- the date;
- findings;
- required actions;
- disposition;
- outcome;
- lifecycle implications.

## 7. Record Integrity

Published records must not be silently modified or replaced.

Material corrections require, as applicable:

- a correction record;
- a new review record;
- an amendment;
- a superseding acknowledgment;
- another documented lifecycle action.

Historical review and Publisher records must remain discoverable.

Repository history may support provenance but does not replace explicit review and acknowledgment records.
