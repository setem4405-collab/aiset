# PROGRAM-0001 Reviews

This directory contains review, verification, Publisher acknowledgment, and related lifecycle records associated with `PROGRAM-0001 — AISET Program Charter`.

The records in this directory preserve the review history, findings, decisions, verification outcomes, and Publisher acknowledgments supporting the evolution of the Charter.

---

## 1. Directory Purpose

This directory provides traceable records for:

- architectural review;
- governance review;
- normative terminology review;
- targeted verification;
- editorial review;
- security and privacy review;
- Publisher lifecycle acknowledgment;
- decision and approval records;
- future independent or external review.

Each record is expected to identify, as applicable:

- the reviewed document;
- the reviewed version;
- the record identifier;
- the review or acknowledgment scope;
- the responsible reviewer or Publisher representative;
- the date;
- findings;
- required actions;
- disposition;
- outcome;
- lifecycle implications.

## 6. Record Integrity

Published records must not be silently modified or replaced.

Material corrections require, as applicable:

- a correction record;
- a new review record;
- an amendment;
- a superseding acknowledgment;
- another documented lifecycle action.

Historical review and Publisher records must remain discoverable.

Repository history may support provenance but does not replace explicit review and acknowledgment records.

## 7. Role Disclosure

During the foundation stage, the Program Originator may also perform multiple roles, including:

- Author;
- Lead Editor;
- Reviewer;
- Repository Maintainer;
- Registry Administrator;
- Publisher Representative.

This overlap must be disclosed in applicable records.

Internal records must not be represented as:

- independent external review;
- third-party certification;
- external standards validation;
- legal advice.
