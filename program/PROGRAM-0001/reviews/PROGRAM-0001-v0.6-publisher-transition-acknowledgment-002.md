## 6. Verified Normative Correction

`PROGRAM-0001-REVIEW-003` identified one blocking normative operator issue in the Technology Independence clause.

`PROGRAM-0001 v0.5 Draft` corrected the clause to state:

> A normative AISET entity **MUST NOT** depend on a specific technology, model, executor, programming language, runtime, vendor, or implementation framework.

`PROGRAM-0001-REVIEW-004` verified that:

- the required correction was applied;
- the clause now expresses an explicit normative prohibition;
- the correction is consistent with the normative keyword policy;
- version traceability is preserved;
- return to formal Review status is permitted.

The Publisher acknowledges the verified correction as the immediate basis for the transition to `PROGRAM-0001 v0.6 Review`.
