# PROGRAM-0001 Brand Identity and Technical Identifier Decision 004

**Program:** PROGRAM-0001  
**Decision ID:** PROGRAM-0001-DECISION-004  
**Status:** Approved  
**Decision authority:** Human Program Authority  
**Authorization:** AISET-PHASE0-001K-BRAND-DECISION  
**Decision date:** 2026-07-22

## Decision

The canonical public brand of the program is **AISET CFS**.

The canonical expansion used for human-facing brand communication is
**Cognitive Framing System**.

The canonical technical identifier is `aiset-cfs`.

The hyphenated public form `AISET-CFS` MUST NOT be used. A hyphen is permitted
only inside the lowercase technical identifier `aiset-cfs` where a space is
unsuitable or unavailable.

The standalone form **AISET** MAY be used only after the full public brand has
been defined and only where the context unambiguously refers to AISET CFS.

The canonical program domain is `aisetcfs.com`.

## Architecture terminology

**Cognitive Context Transformations** remains a technical architectural
concept. It is not the public brand name and does not replace the human-facing
expansion **Cognitive Framing System**.

This decision does not rename the existing architectural identifiers `OCIA`,
`CCO`, `CT`, `CTSpec`, `CPC`, `CPTP`, or `CEF`.

## Trademark clearance requirement

Before any trademark application or claim of legal availability, a separate
trademark-clearance activity MUST assess **AISET CFS** and relevant similar
marks through EUIPO, WIPO, and relevant national registers.

The initial goods-and-services analysis MUST consider Nice Classes 9, 41, and
42. Classes 35 and 16 MUST be considered only after the actual intended goods
and services have been confirmed.

The clearance MUST specifically assess the potentially conflicting EUIPO mark
**NovAIset**, application number `019185113`, including similarity and overlap
in the relevant goods and services.

This decision does not state or imply that **AISET CFS**, `AISET`, or any
related designation is legally available, registrable, or free from conflict.

## Scope and non-authorizations

This decision records brand identity and the technical identifier only. It
does not authorize:

- filing or registering a trademark;
- renaming a repository, package, domain, branch, or existing artifact;
- bulk replacement of terminology in existing documents;
- changing the names of OCIA or its architectural entities; or
- any legal representation concerning ownership, availability, or
  non-infringement.

Each such action requires a separate AISET Galley gate and, where applicable,
qualified legal review.
