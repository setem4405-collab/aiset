## 3. Independence

The appointed authority confirms that she acts independently from:

- the Program Originator;
- the Approval Authority.

The appointed authority is not subordinate to those authorities for appeal and reconsideration decisions under PROGRAM-0001.

---

## 4. Authority

The Appeal and Reconsideration Authority may:

- confirm a decision of the Approval Authority;
- reverse a decision of the Approval Authority;
- return a decision for reconsideration;
- require appointment of a new Approval Authority where conflict, loss of independence, or inability to continue exists.

---

## 5. Conflict-of-interest Obligations

The appointed authority must disclose any actual, potential, or perceived conflict of interest.

The appointed authority must not consider an appeal concerning her own decision.

Where conflict, recusal, or unavailability exists, another independent person must be temporarily appointed.

---

## 6. Scope Limitation

The appointment is limited to:

- appeals;
- reconsideration of approval decisions;
- conflicts of interest;
- related procedural review under PROGRAM-0001.

The appointment does not automatically grant authority over authorship, editing, repository maintenance, registry administration, Publisher decisions, or unrelated governance matters.

---
