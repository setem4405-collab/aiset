## 1. Purpose

This record designates an Approval Authority distinct from the Program Originator for the first Approved Publication decision concerning:

`PROGRAM-0001 — AISET Program Charter`

This designation does not approve the publication and does not predetermine the outcome of any future approval decision.

---

## 2. Scope of Authority

Evgeny Dunaev is authorized to make the final Approval Authority decision for the first proposed transition of `PROGRAM-0001` from Candidate Publication to Approved Publication.

Within that scope, the Approval Authority may:

1. approve the proposed Approved Publication;
2. reject the proposed Approved Publication;
3. defer the decision pending additional evidence or governance arrangements;
4. return the publication for revision;
5. require additional review or clarification;
6. identify blocking or non-blocking findings;
7. require correction of lifecycle, integrity, registry, Publisher, provenance, licensing, or conflict-of-interest records.

The Approval Authority is not required to approve the publication. A positive decision is not expected, promised, or predetermined.

---

## 3. Limitations

This designation does not authorize Evgeny Dunaev to:

- modify the canonical Charter artifact directly;
- act as Author or Lead Editor of PROGRAM-0001;
- alter historical review or publication records;
- issue registry records;
- issue Publisher acknowledgments;
- approve a materially different publication without a new or amended authority record;
- delegate this authority without a separate canonical delegation record;
- use the role for any publication other than the first Approved Publication decision for PROGRAM-0001;
- waive applicable independence, conflict-of-interest, integrity, review, or publication requirements.

Registry administration and Publisher authorization remain separate functions.

## 6. Duration and Termination

This appointment applies only to the first Approved Publication decision concerning PROGRAM-0001.

The appointment terminates upon the earliest of:

1. publication of the canonical final decision within scope;
2. written withdrawal by Evgeny Dunaev before the final decision;
3. documented recusal due to conflict of interest;
4. documented revocation for cause;
5. documented replacement by a valid successor authority;
6. abandonment or supersession of the proposed first Approved Publication pathway.
