## Purpose

DISCOVERY-0001 documents the engineering discoveries that led to the creation of the AISET Program and the emergence of the AISET Foundation Architecture.

The publication answers one fundamental question:

> Why did AISET emerge?

It explains the progression from engineering observations and gaps to discovery evolution, architectural convergence, Foundation Architecture Emergence and the resulting Discovery Principles.
